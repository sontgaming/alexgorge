About me

Alex Gorge

I've been a photographer since my freshman year of high school, and over the past 15 years, I've shot more than 750 weddings, many of which have been featured in some amazing magazines.

It's a privilege to witness the love shared by my couples and transform their memories into art. My style is classic, airy, and deeply romantic. I blend digital and film to create modern yet timeless portraits. When I'm not at a wedding, you can find me serving at my local church, brunching with my wife, or spending time with my nephews.
